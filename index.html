How it Works

In order place an order on Maariyaan restaurant you need to visit our website using
https://kumarwayadande.github.io./mr2/ url.

01 Visit Website

Visit our website using above url. Afterwards you have to enter the pincode of area in which you are residing. When you enter it then you will be redirected to next page.

02 Select Menu

once you come on this page you will encounter some list of available food items in your area defined by pincode you entered. Then you have to select what you want to order.

03 Wait for Delivery

Once you clicked on a food item then you will be redirected to a page where you can pay all the bill. Then you have to wait until your order is delivered.
